To be or not to be [a writer], is the duality I foster within.
Hey there, I am Saurav, wait let me quickly cd to /usr/bin/
and execute ./greetings.sh
a standard mundane file, with "hello world" echoed out loud,
followed by a bunch of gibberish, all meaningless but allowed;
- such is life, till we perish.
